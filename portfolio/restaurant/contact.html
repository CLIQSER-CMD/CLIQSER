Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a dress code at Velora Luxe? expand_more
Yes, to maintain our exclusive fine dining environment, we enforce a Smart Elegant dress code. We respectfully request that guests refrain from wearing athletic apparel, sand-shoes, torn denim, or casual shorts. Jackets are recommended for gentlemen dining in the Main Hall and Private Vaults.
Can you accommodate severe dietary restrictions? expand_more
Absolutely. Our kitchen can adjust courses for gluten-free, dairy-free, vegetarian, or vegan requirements. Please specify your restrictions in detail when filling out the reservation form, or notify our concierge at least 24 hours prior to your seating.
Is valet parking available? expand_more
Yes, Velora Luxe offers complimentary valet parking services at the main entrance. Security-monitored garage storage is used for all vehicles. Simply pull up to our front portico upon arrival.
Do you host corporate summits or private events? expand_more
Yes. The Private Stone Vaults (up to 20 guests) and the Glass Greenhouse (up to 40 guests) can be reserved for exclusive private dining, corporate retreats, or celebratory banquets. Please contact our event coordination desk at events@veloradining.com for customization menus and pricing.
